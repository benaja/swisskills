Material provided

Your employer got some material from the customer. You are provided with several images and five color schemes.

Color scheme: You have to use one of the provided color schemes.

Material location: The material is located under /material/part_two/.

Material overview: There is a material overview page, where you can find an overview of all material provided by the customer.

Total points: 86

Task 1 - Logo design

Your first task is to create a new logo for the startup using your favorite tool (e.g. Adobe Illustrator). They wish that from the logo you can easily identify their name "nate.shure.io". Also it should be easy to recognize their main topic "nature conservancy". Therefore you can use plants, trees and flowers in the logo. They want to leave it up to you, since you were the specialist, they say.

The customer asked to get the RAW design file and also a PNG version which he can print and show his stakeholders.

  • Expected result

    The RAW logo file and PNG file, placed under work/part_two/logo/logo.*

Marking scheme

Objective 4

  • 1 The logo files are placed under /work/part_two/logo/
  • 1 The logo uses the text "nate.shure.io"
  • 1 The logo consists of either one of the following elements: plant(s), tree(s), flower(s) OR the slogan "Say It With Nature".
  • 1 The design uses only one of the five color schemes provided

Subjective 15

  • 6 Topic: The logo design matches the topic
  • 4 Impression: Colors used and theme are appropriate for the target audience
  • 3 Creativity: Design is creative (1), suprising (1) and innovative (1)
  • 2 Graphic work: Graphics are prepared neatly and integrated professionally

Task 2 - WebApp design

Create a WebApp design using your favorite tool (e.g. Adobe Photoshop). The design has to meet professional visual expectations and to implement one of the five provided color schemes.

  • Screen dimensions

    You have to design for the Samsung Galaxy S5 screen only:

    • 1920x1080px
    • Portrait format only!

    Use the Photoshop template file (template.psd) provided.

The following pages have to be designed:

  • Splash page: The customer wants to have a splash page (entry page). Design the splash page which is attractive and modern. The customer asked to use pictures on the splash page to make it more appealing to his customers.

    Place the files under work/part_two/design/splash.*

    Navigation: Design the navigation. The navigation consists of the following Items:

    • Home
    • Schedule
    • Speakers
    • Application Form

    Place the files under work/part_two/design/start.*

    Schedule: Design the schedule page containing the following:

    • The slogan: "Say It With Nature"
    • The schedule (Morning and afternoon separately, the afternoon details are optional)
    • Schedule items:
      • Start and end time
      • Name
      • Type (workshop, talk)

    Use the schedule data in schedule.txt and place the files under work/part_two/design/schedule.*

    Speakers list: Design the speakers list containing the following:

    • Title
    • At least two speakers with:
      • Image
      • Name
      • Short description

    Use the speaker photos (*.jpg), their short descriptions (*.txt) in the speakers-folder and place the files under work/part_two/design/speakers.*

    Application form: Design the application form containing the following:

    • Title
    • Application form with
      • First name
      • Last name
      • Email
      • Submit button
      • Example error message

    Place the files under work/part_two/design/application.*

The customer asked to get the RAW design file and also a PNG version which he can print and show his stakeholders.

  • Expected result

    The five designs (RAW and JPG), placed under work/part_two/design/

Marking scheme

Objective 26

  • 1 The design files are placed under /work/part_two/design/
  • 1 The design uses only one of the five color schemes provided
  • 4 The splash page looks different from the standard page (1) and implements the logo (1), the name (1) and at least one image (1).
  • 1 The navigational items are placed in logical way according to their relevance
  • 6 The schedule page implements the slogan "Say It With Nature" (1) and the schedule given in schedule.txt (1) displaying the start and end time (1), name (1) and type (1). The morning and afternoon are displayed seperatly (1).
  • 5 The speakers page implements the title (1), at least two speakers(1) which contain the speakers name (1), image (1) and a short description (1) used from the speakers/ folder.
  • 6 The application form contains the title (1), the input fields: first name (1), last name (1), email (1), submit button (1) and an example error message (1).
  • 2 The logo is displayed on all pages (1). A title (heading) is displayed on all pages - except on the splash page (1).

Subjective 41

  • 6 Impression: Colors used and theme are appropriate for the target audience
  • 6 Composition: Design elements fit together and are relevant
  • 6 Creativity: Design is creative (2), suprising (2) and innovative (2)
  • 6 Graphic work: Pictures are prepared neatly and integrated professionally
  • 6 Usability: The website design is user friendly, efficient and engaging (2). It is clear on which page I am (1). It is clearly recognizable where to find the navigational items (1). All interaction elements can be easily identified and their purpose is obvious (2).
  • 9 Readability: The text is well readable: contrast (1), font-size (1) and line-height (1). Inner and outer distances to their adjacent elements shows at what element the text belongs (3). Text is readable by color-blind readers (e.g. distinction between red and green) (3).

The website has to meet professional expectations - visually and technically.
